App Data Collection and Permissions

To provide accurate credit evaluation services, the Application collects the following categories of data. All data is encrypted during transmission and storage, used solely for credit assessment and risk modeling, and will never be shared with third parties.

1. Personal Information You Provide

During the credit evaluation process, you may be asked to provide the following personal information to help us build your financial profile:

  • Residence Type — Your current housing status (e.g., owned, rented), used to assess financial stability.
  • Education — Your highest education level, as a factor in repayment capacity analysis.
  • Religion — Demographic information for compliance and risk modeling.
  • Marital Status — Used to evaluate household financial obligations.
  • Number of Dependents — Helps assess your financial responsibility and expense burden.
  • Employment Type — Whether you are salaried, self-employed, or otherwise, to gauge income stability.
  • Employment Duration — Length of time in your current job, indicating career stability.
  • Monthly Income — Your monthly earnings, a core factor in determining credit limits.
  • Daily Expense — Your estimated daily spending, used with income to assess repayment capability.
  • Occupation — Your profession type, used for income and stability modeling.
  • Company Position — Your role level, reflecting career progression and earning potential.
  • Loan Purpose — The intended use of the credit, supporting responsible lending practices.

2. Identity Verification Data

To verify your identity and prevent fraud, the Application requires you to upload a photo of your official government-issued ID (PAN Card or Aadhaar Card, front side only). The uploaded image is used for:

  • Confirming your true identity
  • Cross-checking with national databases to verify authenticity
  • Preventing identity theft and fraudulent applications

All ID images are encrypted during transmission and storage, and are never shared with third parties.

3. Location Data

The Application collects your device's location to:

  • Verify your city matches your application and prevent fraud by detecting unusual sign-ins
  • Support geolocation-based features and content
  • Analyze aggregated usage patterns to improve the Application

Location data is encrypted, used solely for risk assessment, and never shared.

4. Camera and Photo Permission

The camera permission is used exclusively for capturing your ID document during identity verification. We only access the camera when you actively choose to take a photo. We do not access your photo gallery except for the specific ID image you select to upload.

5. Contact List Data

You may choose to upload selected contacts from your address book. This data is used for:

  • Building your social credit graph
  • Analyzing the credit distribution in your social circle
  • Assessing your social stability

Contact upload is optional. Good social network relationships, such as contacts with generally higher credit scores, will indirectly improve your credit score. This process strictly follows privacy principles and is used only for model calculation.

6. Installed Application List

The Application collects information about the apps installed on your device. This data is used for credit risk modeling and fraud prevention:

  • Analyze financial behavior patterns (e.g., presence of other financial or lending apps)
  • Calculate a more precise credit score based on your overall app usage profile

App list data is collected only for credit risk assessment and is encrypted and processed securely. It is never shared with third parties.

7. Device and Hardware Information

For credit risk modeling and fraud prevention, the Application collects certain device and hardware information, including:

  • Device Model and Manufacturer — To identify device type and assess device-related risk
  • Operating System and OS Version — For compatibility and security assessment
  • Device Unique Identifier (e.g., IMEI/Android ID) — To verify device uniqueness and prevent multi-account fraud
  • IP Address — For network-based fraud detection and geolocation verification
  • Screen Resolution and DPI — For UI optimization and device fingerprinting
  • Battery Level and Charging Status — To analyze device usage patterns as part of behavioral risk profiling
  • Available Storage and Memory — For app performance optimization

All device and hardware data is encrypted during transmission and storage, used solely for risk assessment, fraud prevention, and service improvement.
