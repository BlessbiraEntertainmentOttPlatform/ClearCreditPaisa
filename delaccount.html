Account Deletion

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your ClearCreditPaisa account and all associated data directly within the App. Please follow the steps below:

1
On the main screen, tap the Mine tab (the third tab at the bottom).
2
Tap Settings.
3
Scroll down and tap Delete My Account.
4
Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you delete your account:

  • All personal information, identity documents, credit evaluation data, and usage records associated with your account will be permanently erased.
  • You will be logged out immediately and returned to the start screen.
  • This action cannot be undone. You will not be able to recover any data after deletion.
